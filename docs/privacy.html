Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Free ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI agent monitoring and control application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Free collects only what is necessary to operate the service:

  • Account credentials — email address used to sign in.
  • Session data — AI agent messages and outputs, transmitted end-to-end encrypted. The relay server cannot read the content of your sessions.
  • Diagnostic logs — optional, stored locally on your device. Never uploaded without your explicit action.

End-to-End Encryption

All session content (messages, tool calls, file changes) is encrypted on your device using X25519 key exchange and AES-256-GCM before transmission. Our relay server only forwards ciphertext and has no access to the plaintext of your data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your session content for advertising or model training.
  • We do not track your location.

Self-Hosting

Free supports self-hosted relay servers. If you operate your own server, this privacy policy applies only to the mobile app. You are responsible for the data handling practices of your self-hosted instance.

Data Retention

Session data is retained on the relay server to support cross-device sync. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's Settings screen.

Children's Privacy

Free is intended for developers and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
